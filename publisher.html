Product overview

Built for an accessible first play—and a stronger second one.

The core loop is immediately demonstrable: draw seven tiles, decide what to keep, spend limited discards and score the best qualifying combination.

Clear market position

7FALL™ sits between familiar combination-building games and light push-your-luck strategy. Players understand the visible goals quickly, while Wilds, Breaks and discard timing create depth.

Flexible production path

The current physical prototype establishes the mechanic. Component form, visual identity and packaging remain adaptable to a publishing partner’s line and manufacturing targets.

Digital proof of play

The browser edition allows immediate remote evaluation and captures gameplay events such as starts, rounds, completions and new-game behavior.

Replay-friendly structure

Short sessions, random draws and personal-best tracking encourage repeat play without requiring a campaign, account or long setup.
